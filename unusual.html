The Mood Ring

The people of this world are like a mood ring; their eyes, hair, and skin will change colors with the changing
of their emotional state. Jadzia's base is purple and blue but when stressed her hair turns to bright red, eyes shift to gold, and her skin has a an
array of pink hues. When she is seeking solitude she becomes various shades of blue; hair electric, skin baby, and eyes navy. Everyone has their own color scheme.
