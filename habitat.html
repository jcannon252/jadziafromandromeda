Two Suns City

In the Andromeda galaxy a special planet floats of the edge of the spiral galaxy. With a colorful array of rocks and vegetation.
The grasses are opalescent and change color depending on the time of the day and shine from the two suns on the surface. The oceans are bright and clear.
The people of this planet are peacefufl and curious; exploration and the desire to understand the universe is their driving force of life.
They long to connect to their home world as well as the universe at large. The planet provides all that is necessary for the people to live and expand their horizons.
Fruits grow on trees in the streets, everywhere is useful to the people and everything is open.
