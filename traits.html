The Magical Traits

Jadzia in thin, exceptionally tall, around seven feet, like the rest of the people of her world.They glide as they
walk almost as if they were floating an inch above the ground. She is curious, driven, and suprisingly quirky.
Drawn to creativity she thinks outside the box. She often finds herself in sticky situations when traveling to other worlds but is clever enough to find her way out.
