Who is Jadzia?

Born in the Andromeda Glaxay on the planet with two suns. Jadzia is the child of two scientist, exploreres, who travel the stars and celebrate the land they were born to. Jadzia is tall and long with purple and blue hair, she is curious, ambitious, and a little strange. She often finds herself in sticky situations but her cleverness always finds a way for her avoid any real danger.Her connection with nature is strong, she explores space often but will always find her way back home to reconnect with the land.
